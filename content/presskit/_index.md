  <h2 id="history">KEY FEATURES</h2>
  <strong>Assemble your team</strong>

* Choose from a varied cast of heroes. You can stick with the classics, like a tanky paladin, an archer raining death from afar and a rogue for some sneaky shenaningans behind enemy lines. Or mix things up: Let a sultry vampire charm your foes, set them ablaze with dragonbreath or confuse them with... a jack in the box?
* Level up and upgrade your heroes to unleash their full potential. Choose your favorites wisely as resources are scarce.
* Someone not pulling their weight? Swap them out to try something different.

<strong>Dress for success</strong>

* Discover hundreds of unique items and relics that transform your build into something gloriously broken.
* Every foe has its flaws, and it's your job to exploit them. Those mushroom guys? They look quite flammable. Equipping your barbarian with the fireblade might ensure a crispy victory.
* Mix and match items to uncover wild synergies. Turn your whole team into snails to supercharge your snailblade-wielding paladin. Grow stronger by hoarding gold with greed-based relics. Wield cursed items for immense power… while the rest of your team pays the price

<strong>Strategize and control the battlefield</strong>

* Your heroes have minds of their own, so the moment the fight starts, they will do whatever they think is best. Free will, huh? But don’t be fooled: the real decisions happen before the battle even begins. Composition and positioning of your team is what truly defines the outcome
* Deploy your team freely across the battlefield, but remember that the enemies will be attacking from all sides as well! Consider your options, adapt your strategy or face the consequences - if your whole team gets wiped out, the run is over!
* Remember to hydrate - efficient use of your potion slots can turn your luck around. Pick from diverse flavours such as emergency healing, immediately setting the enemies on fire or even summoning some helpful slimes!

<strong>Explore the dungeon</strong>

* Navigate the dungeon by choosing between multiple paths, each leading either to combat, a shop, a camp, or a special event.
* Play it safe or welcome the challenge - the choice is yours. But remember, the tougher the room, the greater the rewards to strengthen your team.
* Watch your progress carefully. Every step deeper into the dungeon gets you closer to the boss fight, so better be prepared!
